**Secondary Diagnoses:**

- **Hyperlipidemia, Well-Controlled**
- **Fatigue and Weight Loss (Unspecified Cause):** Likely related to the infectious process and constitutional symptoms. Monitor for resolution as pneumonia improves.

- **Sputum Culture and Gram Stain:** Continue pending results.
- **Chest CT Scan (if not contraindicated):** Consider for further evaluation of consolidation or suspicion of underlying malignancy, especially given the patient's weight loss and persistent fatigue.
- **HIV, HBV, HCV Serologies:** Already negative.
- **Arthrocentesis (if joint symptoms persist):** Consider for joint fluid analysis to rule out infectious arthritis or autoimmune arthropathy.
- **Thyroid Function Tests (TSH, free T4):** Given the patient's fatigue and weight loss, to rule out hypothyroidism.

- **Continue Amlodipine 5 mg daily** for hypertension.
- **Continue Atorvastatin 40 mg nightly** for hyperlipidemia.
- **Monitor for Drug Interactions:** Levoquin (levofloxacin) may potentiate anticoagulants and may have interactions with other medications. No current anticoagulation or antiplatelet therapy in use.
- **Discontinue OTC Pain Medications:** Avoid NSAIDs due to the risk of gastrointestinal bleeding, especially with concurrent antibiotic use.

**5. Follow-Up:**
- **Follow-up with Primary Care Physician:** After discharge to monitor for resolution of symptoms and ensure that the patient is not developing complications such as sepsis, pleural effusion, or respiratory failure.
- **Referral to Infectious Disease (if needed):** If sputum culture results are positive or if the patient is not responding to initial therapy, consider consult for further management.
- **Referral to Rheumatology (if arthralgia persists):** If joint symptoms do not improve with resolution of the infection, consider autoimmune or inflammatory conditions.

**6. Patient Education:**
- **Infection Control:** Advise the patient to practice good hand hygiene and avoid close contact with sick individuals.
- **Nutrition:** Encourage a high-protein, high-calorie diet to support recovery.
- **Rest and Activity:** Encourage adequate rest and gradual return to activities as tolerated.
- **Medication Adherence:** Emphasize the importance of completing the full course of antibiotics to prevent recurrence and antibiotic resistance.
